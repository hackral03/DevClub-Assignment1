COMMON TIPS

You can prevent infection or serious illness by following these simple tips:
  • Wear a mask.



  • Keep atleast a distance of 6ft from people in public.



  • Frequently wash your hands with soap or use alcohol-based sanitizer.



  • If you are elligible get your vaccine doses at the earliest.
